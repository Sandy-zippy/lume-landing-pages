No. The membership is non-refundable. That's why we have a discovery conversation first — to ensure mutual fit before you commit.

No. The membership is ₹1,18,000 as a one-time annual payment. We accept UPI, bank transfer, cheque, or cash.

No. We don't track attendance. But members who engage and show up get the most value from the community.
