of the conversation
The best connectors listen 70% and talk 30%. Most people do the opposite.
@thelumeproject
Caption
The 70/30 rule changed how I connect with people. Listen 70%. Talk 30%. Sounds simple. Almost no one does it. Watch any networking event. Most people are waiting for their turn to talk. Planning what to say next. Looking over your shoulder for someone more important. The people who stand out? They ask questions. They follow up on your answers. They make you feel like the most interesting person in the room. Here's the counterintuitive part: By talking less, they become more memorable. People don't remember what you said. They remember how you made them feel. Try it at your next event: - Ask open-ended questions - Follow up on their answers ("Tell me more about that...") - Resist the urge to relate everything back to yourself 70/30. That's it.
